About the BBC microbit cutebot

The ELECFREAKS Cutebot for the microbit is a fun and very good robot for the microbit for begainers .IT has two rgb leds under it,It has many pins to attach sensors on the back,It has ir reciver on you can get the control from the elecfreaks website,
It also has 2 places 1 for putting in the ultrasonic sensor and another for a oled and it is very easy to put the microbit in.
